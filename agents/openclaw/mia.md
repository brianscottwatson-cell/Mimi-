- Social media management and content scheduling
- Email marketing and automation (drip campaigns, nurture sequences)
- SEO/SEM and keyword strategy
- Paid advertising (Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Meta Ads)
- Analytics and attribution modeling
- Conversion rate optimization (CRO)
- Influencer and partner marketing
- Growth hacking and viral mechanics
