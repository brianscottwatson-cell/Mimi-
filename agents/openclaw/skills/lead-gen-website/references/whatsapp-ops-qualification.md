# WhatsApp Operations & Lead Qualification

## Pre-filled Message Strategy
Use WhatsApp links with pre-filled messages to reduce friction and identify lead source:

```
https://wa.me/33XXXXXXXXX?text=Bonjour%2C%20je%20souhaite%20un%20devis%20pour%20[SERVICE]%20a%20[VILLE]
```

Customize per page:
- Homepage: generic inquiry
- Service page: service-specific message
- Ad landing page: include campaign reference

## Lead Qualification Flow

### Step 1: Auto-greeting (manual or WhatsApp Business)
"Bonjour ! Merci de nous contacter. Pour vous repondre au mieux, pouvez-vous preciser :"
1. Quel service vous interesse ?
2. Votre ville / code postal ?
3. C'est urgent ou vous planifiez ?

### Step 2: Qualify (within 5 minutes)
- **Hot lead**: Urgent need + in service area → call immediately
- **Warm lead**: Planning + in service area → schedule callback
- **Cold lead**: Out of area or just browsing → polite redirect

### Step 3: Convert
- Hot: "Je vous appelle dans 2 minutes pour en discuter ?"
- Warm: "Quand etes-vous disponible pour qu'on en parle ? Je vous envoie un creneau."
- Cold: "Malheureusement, nous n'intervenons pas dans votre secteur. Voici [alternative]."

## Response Time Benchmarks
- **Target**: Under 5 minutes during business hours
- **Acceptable**: Under 30 minutes
- **Losing leads**: Over 1 hour
- After hours: auto-reply with next availability

## WhatsApp Business Features to Use
- **Business profile**: Complete with hours, address, website
- **Quick replies**: Pre-written responses for common questions
- **Labels**: Organize conversations (New, Qualified, Devis envoi, Client)
- **Catalog**: Show services with prices (if applicable)
- **Away message**: Auto-reply outside business hours

## Tracking
- Log all WhatsApp leads in CRM/spreadsheet
- Track: source (which page/ad), service requested, location, outcome
- Monthly: count leads, conversion rate, average response time
