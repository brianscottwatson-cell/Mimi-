# Conversion Best Practices — Local Lead Gen

## CTA Strategy
- **Primary CTA**: Phone call (highest intent, fastest conversion)
- **Secondary CTA**: WhatsApp (lower friction for younger demographics)
- **Tertiary CTA**: Contact form (captures details for follow-up)
- Place CTAs above the fold, after key content sections, and in sticky mobile bar
- Use action-oriented text: "Appeler maintenant", "Devis gratuit", "Demander un rappel"

## Contact Options (Priority Order)
1. **Click-to-call** — direct phone link, always visible on mobile
2. **WhatsApp** — pre-filled message with service context
3. **Contact form** — minimal fields (name, phone, service, message)
4. **Email** — fallback for formal inquiries

## Trust Signals
- Years of experience / number of interventions
- Customer reviews/testimonials (real names, specific details)
- Professional certifications and insurance
- "Devis gratuit et sans engagement"
- Response time guarantee ("Reponse sous 2h")
- Service area clarity ("Nous intervenons a [city] et 30km autour")

## Form Optimization
- Maximum 4-5 fields (name, phone required; email, service, message optional)
- Phone field first (highest-value data point)
- Clear privacy consent checkbox linking to privacy policy
- Success message with expected next step and timeline
- Auto-capture UTM parameters for attribution

## Mobile Optimization
- Sticky bottom CTA bar (Call + WhatsApp buttons)
- Tap targets minimum 48x48px
- Forms usable with thumb only
- No horizontal scroll
- Fast load time (< 3s on 3G)

## Page-Specific Conversion Tips

### Homepage
- Hero with clear value proposition + primary CTA within 3 seconds
- Service overview cards linking to detail pages
- Social proof section (reviews, logos, stats)

### Service Pages
- Problem → Solution → CTA flow
- Pricing transparency (ranges or "devis gratuit")
- FAQ section addressing objections
- Related services cross-links

### Blog/Content Pages
- Contextual CTAs within content (not just sidebar)
- "Besoin d'aide ?" callout box mid-article
- Related service page links

## Attribution & Tracking
- UTM parameters on all inbound links (ads, social, email)
- Form captures: utm_source, utm_campaign, utm_adset, utm_ad
- Phone tracking: unique numbers per channel (if budget allows)
- WhatsApp: pre-filled messages include campaign reference
