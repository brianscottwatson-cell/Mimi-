# RGPD/GDPR Compliance Guide

## Cookie Banner (Required)
- Must appear on first visit before any non-essential cookies are set
- Two clear options: "Accepter" and "Refuser" (no dark patterns)
- Link to cookie policy for details
- Store consent in localStorage (not a cookie, to avoid chicken-and-egg)
- If refused: no analytics, no tracking pixels, no marketing cookies
- Re-show banner if consent expires (recommended: 13 months)

## Privacy Policy Page (Required)
Must include:
- **Identity of data controller**: company name, address, contact
- **Data collected**: list all data types (name, email, phone, IP, cookies)
- **Purpose of processing**: why each data type is collected
- **Legal basis**: consent, legitimate interest, or contract
- **Data retention period**: how long data is kept
- **Third parties**: who receives data (analytics, hosting, CRM)
- **User rights**: access, rectification, deletion, portability, objection
- **How to exercise rights**: contact email/form
- **DPO contact**: if applicable

## Cookie Policy Page (Required)
- List all cookies by category (essential, analytics, marketing)
- For each: name, purpose, duration, provider
- Explain how to manage cookies in browser settings
- Link to privacy policy

## Legal Mentions Page (Required in France)
- Company legal name, form (SARL, SAS, auto-entrepreneur)
- SIRET/SIREN number
- Registered address
- Contact information (phone, email)
- Publication director name
- Hosting provider (name, address)
- If applicable: RCS registration, TVA number

## Forms
- Consent checkbox required before submission
- Link to privacy policy near checkbox
- Clear purpose statement ("Vos donnees sont utilisees uniquement pour traiter votre demande")
- Don't pre-check the consent box
- Store consent proof (timestamp, IP, form version)

## Data Security
- HTTPS on all pages
- Form data transmitted encrypted
- No sensitive data in URLs or logs
- Access controls on stored personal data
- Backup and recovery procedures

## User Rights Implementation
- Contact method clearly listed (email preferred)
- Respond within 30 days maximum
- Free of charge (first request)
- Identity verification before processing
- Document all requests and responses
